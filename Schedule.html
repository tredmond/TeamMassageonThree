Massage therapy is the scientific manipulation of the soft tissues of the body for the purpose of normalizing those tissues and consists of manual techniques that include applying fixed or movable pressure, holding, and/or causing movement of or to the body.

    Serenity Lava Shell Massage

    Lava Shell Massage is a relaxing massage that uses heat, like hot stone massage therapy. During a Lava Shell Massage, the therapist uses real shells from the Philippines to gently massage your body enabling the heat to penetrate deeper into the muscles for relaxation. It reduces the muscle tension and stiffness, increases the ease and efficiency of movement and enhances the health and nourishment of skin
